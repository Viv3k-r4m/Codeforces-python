1) Wrong subtraction
a,b=map(int,input().split())
while(b!=0):
    if(a%10==0):
        a//=10
    else:
        a-=1
    b-=1

2) Word
a=input().strip()
b=0
c=0
for i in a:
    if i.islower():
        b+=1
    elif i.isupper():
        c+=1
if(b>=c):
    print(a.lower())
else:
    print(a.upper())

3) Soldier and Bananas
